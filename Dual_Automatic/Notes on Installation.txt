Motors are using 3 phase 240volt.

The control wiring and motor control was more complicated than I felt comfortable messing with, plus an electrician is required for these motor wiring.

I talked with their electrician.

I left a wiring diagram in the box where he should land his wiring to my relays.

Relays 1-2-3 => Common connections - Here you land the source 3 phase for the motors

Relays 4-5-6 => NC connections - Motor Forward wiring
                NO connections - Motor Reverse wiring

The scale operates 2 relays (unlabled).

Relay 7 - When activated sends 120VAC to relays 1-2-3 coils.  When activated relays 1-2-3 will provide 3 phase power to relays 4-5-6.

Relay 8 - When not activated Motor is in forward
          When activated Motor is in reverse

The electricians will swap the NO connections for 2 of the relays to reverse the motor.
